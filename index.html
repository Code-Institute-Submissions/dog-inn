About Us

Who We Are

I have a special bond with dogs that made me decide to bind my whole life with them.
In 2017, I started working as a petsitter, giving my pupils the opportunity to stay in peace under a careful eye during the trips of their owners.
In 2019 i obtained the higher certificate in dog behavior and psychology.
During these few years, various animals have been hosted under my roof. Many dogs grew up under my care. Every time i try to make each of them feel at home. Fascinated by my pupils, I decided to help their owners in their everyday problems by teaching them how to communicate with their sweets. Starting with providing practical knowledge about raising and building a bond with a puppy, by learning basic obedience skills using the clicker method, and ending with solving problem behaviors.
In our hotel, we create a family atmosphere, so the dogs entrusted to us can spend an unforgettable time while their owners are away.

Services

OUR SERVICES

What we offer

24/7 CARE

Home conditions of stay adapted to the most demanding residents. All necessary dog luxuries and 100% of our attention and love. Individual approach to each dog. Implementation of the owner's detailed recommendations.

CARE AT THE OWNER'S HOME

Your dog feels better at home? No problem! We will adapt the care exactly to your needs

PUPPIES ROOM

Has a puppy come to your home? Early socialization under the supervision of a trainer will give you less concern about the future of your pet and will allow you to avoid later behavior problems. We are learning cleanliness, basic commands, good manners and proper relations with other animals.

WALK

One of the most important elements of a dog's day. The level of activity and the type of play will be ideally suited to the temperament, breed and character of your dog.

TRAINING

We offer a wide range of courses that will allow you to build a proper bond with your dog, teach your dog the right behavior and understand his basic needs. Classes are held in a relaxed and stress-free atmosphere, during which we implement the course program. We focus on having fun together.

COMMON ROOM

Does your dog enjoy the company of other pets? Doesn't like being left alone? The home common room will guarantee him a lot of activity and you will have peace of mind that your pet will not get bored during your absence.
