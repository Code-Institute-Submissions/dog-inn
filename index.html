About Us

Who We Are

I have a special bond with dogs that made me decide to bind my whole life with them.
In 2017, I started working as a petsitter, giving my pupils the opportunity to stay in peace under a careful eye during the trips of their owners.
In 2019 i obtained the higher certificate in dog behavior and psychology.
During these few years, various animals have been hosted under my roof. Many dogs grew up under my care. Every time i try to make each of them feel at home. Fascinated by my pupils, I decided to help their owners in their everyday problems by teaching them how to communicate with their sweets. Starting with providing practical knowledge about raising and building a bond with a puppy, by learning basic obedience skills using the clicker method, and ending with solving problem behaviors.
In our hotel, we create a family atmosphere, so the dogs entrusted to us can spend an unforgettable time while their owners are away.
